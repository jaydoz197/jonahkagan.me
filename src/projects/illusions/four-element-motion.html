4-Element Motion Illusions

  • Waves
  • Rotating snakes

4-element motion illusions are illusions consisting of patterns of elements with four different luminance values — one darkest (usually black), one somewhat dark, one somewhat light, and one lightest (usually white). When these elements are arranged in the order darkest —> mid-dark —> lightest —> mid-light, they can create an illusory motion effect in the direction of the arrows.
